An Investigation of MMR‐Related Mumps Cluster Following Immunization Among Practical Nursing Students, Bangkok, Thailand, 2024
Journal of Tropical Medicine (Wiley) · Aug 1, 2025
DOI: 10.1155/jotm/9974081
Show detail

Contributors: Sethapong Lertsakulbunlue, Drunphob Srithammavong, Kamonchanok Tepsittha, Hataya Kanjanasombut, Vitchakorn Poonyakanok, Viravarn Luvira, Phimphan Pisutsan, Rachata Charoenwisedsil, Pathomthep Leowattana, Peeriya Watakulsin et al.

This study investigated a cluster of mumps-like cases among practical nursing students following MMR vaccination, identifying operational risk factors and confirming vaccine-related mumps infections through molecular analysis. Recommendations were made to improve vaccination campaign processes.

Eight deaths were reported, with a gender ratio of 9.25 males per 1 female, and the median age of patients was 39 years.
